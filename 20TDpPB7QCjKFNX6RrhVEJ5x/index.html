Mexican mint

Scientific name :Plectranthus amboinicus
Family: Lamiaceae
Plant Discription and Health Benefits :Commonly known as Ajowain patta and is semi-succulent perennial plant.It is widely used in folk medicine to treat conditions like cold, asthma, constipation, headache, cough, fever and skin diseases. The leaves of the plant are often eaten raw or used as flavoring agents, or incorporated as ingredients in the preparation of traditional food.

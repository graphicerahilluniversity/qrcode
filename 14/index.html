Ashwagandha

Scientific name :Withania somnifera
Family: Solanaceae
Plant Discription and Health Benefits :Ashwagandha is an evergreen shrub. Possible benefits of ashwagandha include reducing stress and anxiety, improving athletic performance, boosting heart health.
